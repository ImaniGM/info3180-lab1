Imani Miller

From the header you would see that my name Imani Miller. It is a quite common name that originated from Swahili and means Faith.
I was born in Kinsgton Jamaica and grew up in the same place. Throughout my childhood I was a very active child and as my mother would tell me,
I was also very mischievous. I attended a few prep schools- St. Andrew Prep and Mona Prep- and one high school- Immaculate Conception High School.
I did fairly well at all these institutions and now I am here at The University of the West Indies pursuing a degree in Computer Science.
Although I do enjoy computer science, i have a few hobbies that one would say are opposites. A few of my hobbies include painting, digital drawing
and nail art.
